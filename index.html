Interesting Facts about Camel

  • Camel's ears are furry.
  • Camel can move easily across the sand because of its specially designed feet.
  • When they find water, they will drink as much as possible.

Interesting Facts about Chameleon

  1. Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder.
  2. Changing skin colour is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  3. Most chameleons have a prehistoric tail that they use to wrap around branches.

Interesting Facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting Facts about Teddy bear

  1. The teddy was named after President Theodore Roosevelt, after he refused to shoot a bear during a 1902 hunting trip.
  2. The correct term for a teddy bear collector is an ‘arctophile’.
  3. A teddy has been into space! Magellan T Bear boarded Space Shuttle Discovery in 1995 as part of a school project.

Interesting Facts about Panda

  • Pandas have great camouflage for their environment.
  • Pandas spend a lot of their day eating.
  • Bamboo is critical to a Panda's diet.

Interesting Facts about Cicada

  1. Cicadas are herbivores.
  2. Cicadas can live a relatively long time: 4 to 17 years.
  3. Cicadas do not sting or bite, according to the University of Michigan.
